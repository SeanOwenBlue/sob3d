FEATURES


COMBO


Most of the derivable clothing on SOB3D is all "combo," which means that they have both a skintight layer and a fit layer on top.



UV MAPS


The UV maps on SOB3D clothing accommodate for the least amount of deforming possible. This means that making cuts using opacity maps and general cloth arrangement are as easy as possible.



HIGH QUALITY


Most of the clothing on SOB3D comes in sets varying in quality.

UV1, mostly applicable to full bodysuits, is currently the lowest quality available of SOB3D. UV2 is an intermediate quality. UV3 is the highest quality offered on SOB3D.

Images shown are UV1 to UV3, from left to right.



REPLICABILITY


Textures and cuts made using one SOB3D shape can easily be copied to other shapes, within the same gender, for similar results.
